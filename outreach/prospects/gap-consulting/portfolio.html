02 Tis the Season Holiday Lighting · Full-Stack Ops Platform Co-owner & Builder
Customer lifecycle platform for a seasonal lighting business — signups, contact intake, scheduling, route management, text comms, invoicing, and payment tracking. Hugo static marketing site on an exe.dev VM with a Go backend; Fillout for yearly signups and a contact form (both validated and spam-filtered into Airtable); TextMagic for customer messaging; Spoke (custom Airtable extension) for route & schedule management; Stripe for invoicing and payments. 03 Authorized Vacuum · Lifecycle Text Automation Full-stack Builder
POS-driven text automation for a retail vacuum dealer. Zapier listens for sale events from the POS, Val.town runs the business logic and persists customers to SQLite, a daily CRON evaluates records and triggers one of two templated messages: a 7-day thank-you & issue-check, or a 365-day annual service reminder. ~200 customers processed per year, ~400 outbound texts , fully unattended — no manual touchpoints required for the dealer to run the program.
